Eligible criteria to apply and required documents

Eligible criteria

  • Nationality : Indian
  • Age : 21 years to 65 years
  • Income : You must have a regular source of income
  • Credit course : 720 or higher

Documents required

  • PAN card
  • Address proof
  • Cancelled cheque
  • Signed ECS mandate
